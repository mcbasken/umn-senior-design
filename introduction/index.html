Digital Design with Programmable Logic

Course Description

Exploring modern digital circuit design using HDLs and FPGAs, integrating AI for personalized learning.

Learning Objectives

  • Remember: Identify digital logic concepts and Verilog syntax.
  • Understand: Explain combinational and sequential circuits.
  • Apply: Implement and simulate FPGA circuits.
  • Analyze: Compare design methodologies.
  • Evaluate: Optimize digital designs.
  • Create: Develop complex FPGA systems.

Final Capstone Project

Design an FPGA-based system with AI-assisted debugging and optimization.
